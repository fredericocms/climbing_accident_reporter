Climbing Accident Reporter

Help improve mountain safety by sharing anonymous accident and incident reports.

What is this?

This is a community-driven tool to report climbing and mountaineering accidents or near-miss incidents in Brazil. Your anonymous input helps climbers learn from past events, prevent future risks, and strengthen safety culture.

Why contribute?

  • 📌 Build a public, searchable database of incidents
  • 🔍 Improve training and awareness for climbers
  • 📈 Share insights without exposing identities
